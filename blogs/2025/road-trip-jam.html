~ Road Trip Jam

My friends Tommaso, Lorenzo, and I, under the name Binary Bucket, have joined a game jam! The event, called Road Trip Jam, challenges participants to create a game based on the theme of a road trip.

For our project, we are developing a narrative adventure game about three siblings going on a journey together. Along the way, players will make choices that shape their relationships and experiences. We wanted to create a game that feels both personal and universally relatable—one that captures the emotions and connections built during a trip.

For us, this game isn’t just about the road trip itself; it’s about the memories made along the way. And in creating it, we’ve made a few of our own.

The game jam deadline is April 26, and we’re excited to bring our vision to life!

Check out the jam here: Road Trip Jam.

~ Niccolò
