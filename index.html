About me

Hi everyone, my name is Niccolò Lucozzi and i am a computer science student at the University of Camerino.
I have been passionate about computers since i was a child, and my interest in technology and computer science led me to choose this university course.
In addition to my passion for computers, i am a great lover of music of all genres, from classical to rock, passing through jazz and pop.
I enjoy experimenting with new sounds and following the concerts of my favorite artists.
I am a curious and passionate person, always looking for new knowledge and challenges. I hope i'll be able to put my passions and skills to good use in my future work.

Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is creating new computer projects, such as websites, mobile applications, and much more.
I am always looking for new ideas and projects to develop, and I enjoy putting my programming and design skills to the test. I have developed several projects both on my own and in collaboration with other students, and every time we complete a new project, I feel proud of the results we achieved.

I am convinced that the creation of computer projects can open up many professional and personal opportunities, and I am enthusiastic about continuing to learn and improve my skills in the field of computer science.

I also enjoy playing videogames, table-top RPGs, and board games.
These activities allow me to have fun with friends and family while also stimulating my strategic and problem-solving skills.
I particularly enjoy the social aspect of table-top RPGs and board games, as they provide a great opportunity to spend time with others and create unique experiences together.
